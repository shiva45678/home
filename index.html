Using the OPD Assistant: A Step-by-Step Guide

1. Input Patient Vitals

  • Enter essential details: age, sex, and chief complaints.
  • Alternatively, provide a concise case summary in free-text format.

2. AI-Assisted History Taking

  • Respond to targeted questions posed by the AI to refine the diagnostic pathway.

3. Integrate Lab Findings

  • Input any positive or significant lab results as prompted or when available.

4. Review Differential Diagnosis

  • The AI presents potential diagnoses with confidence scores. Aim for a high confidence level for the final diagnosis.

5. Formulate Treatment Plan

  • Access an AI-generated, personalized treatment plan based on the confirmed diagnosis.

Navigating the EMG Assistant for Emergencies

1. Provide Patient Data

  • Quickly input age, sex, chief complaints, and critical medical history.

2. Describe Emergency Context

  • Clearly detail the emergency scenario and the patient's current condition.

3. Receive Actionable Insights

  • Get immediate AI-powered recommendations for emergency management and subsequent steps.

Mastering Clinical Skills with the OSCE Simulator

1. Choose a Scenario

  • Select from a diverse library of realistic Objective Structured Clinical Examination scenarios.

2. Interact with AI Patient

  • Engage in simulated patient encounters to practice history taking, physical examination, and communication skills.

3. Obtain Instant Feedback

  • Receive personalized, constructive, and detailed feedback on your performance.

4. Analyze Performance Metrics

  • Review auto-generated mark sheets and performance analytics to identify areas for improvement.

Optimizing Care with the AI Treatment Planner

1. Enter Case Details

  • Input the confirmed diagnosis, relevant patient history, and other pertinent clinical data.

2. Generate Treatment Protocol

  • Receive a comprehensive, day-wise treatment plan, including accurate dosages and administration routes.

3. Clinician Review & Customization

  • Critically review the AI-suggested plan and make necessary adjustments based on your clinical judgment and patient-specific factors.

Leveraging the AI ICU Assistant for Critical Care

1. Input Real-time Patient Data

  • Enter the patient's current condition, vital signs, lab results, and relevant medical history.

2. Access Predictive Analytics

  • Receive AI-driven predictions for potential complications, mortality risk, and other critical outcomes.

3. Guided ICU Management

  • Utilize AI-powered suggestions for managing complex ICU cases and optimizing treatment strategies. Monitor and update data for continuous support.
